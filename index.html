Important Information

What is this calculator for?
This calculator is designed to provide helpful estimates of your energy needs, battery storage requirements, and off-grid solar energy solutions based on typical usage patterns.
How accurate are the estimates?
The results are intended to give you a general idea of what might work for your energy needs. Actual energy consumption may vary due to factors such as weather, location, and individual usage habits.
Can I get a customized solution?
For the most accurate assessment and to ensure the best fit for your situation, we recommend consulting with one of our energy experts or solar solution providers. They can help customize a system specifically for your needs.
Is there a warranty on these calculations?
While we strive to provide accurate and reliable calculations, we cannot guarantee the precision or suitability of the results for every situation. This tool is provided "as is" without any warranties, express or implied.
Why use this calculator?
We've created this tool to make it easier for you to start planning your journey toward energy independence. While it's a great starting point, professional advice will ensure you make the most of your investment.
